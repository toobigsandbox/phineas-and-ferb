Guide's Compass

The "Why" & The Montessori Philosophy.

The Cosmic Task

"The flower is not just decoration. It is a tireless worker. Its task is to secure the future of the species. Without it, the story ends."

The 2nd Plane Mind

The 6-12 child loves Hero Worship. We frame the flower as a clever protagonist who "tricks" the insect to achieve its goal.

Materialized Abstraction

We cannot see the biological urge to reproduce. We use the story of the Flower Market to make that abstract biological drive visible and understandable.

The Prep Room

Checklist for the perfect lesson.

  • Black Paper: Essential for contrast (pollen is yellow/white).
  • White Pencil: To label directly on the black paper.
  • Specimen: Lily or Alstroemeria (Parts are large and distinct).
  • Chart O: Keep face down to start.

Rabbit Holes for the Curious

Botany Fact

The Poinsettia isn't a flower! The red parts are leaves (bracts). The flowers are the tiny yellow dots in the center.

Advanced

Gamopetalous: When petals are fused together (like a Morning Glory). "Gamos" = Marriage.
